What is an ALF?

Assisted Living Facilities:
What You Need to Know

An Assisted Living Facility (ALF) provides housing, personal care, and support services for seniors who need help with daily activities — but want to maintain their independence and dignity.

Residential Care
Private or semi-private apartments in a supportive, community environment — home-like, not institutional.
Personal Care & Support
Help with bathing, dressing, medication management, and daily activities — as much or as little as needed.
Social & Wellness
Meals, activities, transportation, and social programs that keep seniors engaged, healthy, and connected.
